timed piano practice - fifth draft
-----------------------------------
:00 (10 min) warm up + scale

  :00 ( 1 min) - stretch and breath
  :01 ( 1 min) - weight lifting
  :02 ( 8 min) - DOY % 3
               = O: 2 octave scales
                 1: contradictory motion
                 2: hanon

:10 (10 min) chord practice
  :10 ( 2 min) DOY % 3 = broken 7th chords, triad run, 7th run
               DOY % 2 = direction
               DOY % 3 = chord type triad, 7th, 9th

  :12 ( 8 min) chords for the day (song or blues)
               - different triad inversions on each hand
               - rhythm across the notes of both hands
               - 7th chords both same
               - 7th chords lh 1, rh 3-5-7 or 3-7
               - 9th chords

               - LH sustain chord
               - LH sustain chord
               - LH play root, then other notes alternating
               - LH Alberti bass - bottom, top, middle, top (any inversion)
               - LH swing bass (pinky low 8vb root, followed by chord - any inversion)
               - LH alternating bass (swing bass, but alternate b/w root and 5th w/pinky)
               - LH arpeggios - root, 5th, 3rd-8va, 5th
               - LH 6th chord - break up with root, 5th, 6th, 5th
               - LH 6th chord - break up with root, 6th, 5th, 6th
               - LH - break up with root + 5th, m3rd, 3rd
               - LH - boogie pattern - 1 8 3 4 4+ 5-8vb
               - LH - classic boogie - 1 3 5 6 m7 6 5 3 1 - broken octaves

               - RH - repeat chord up the keyboard through inversions
               - RH - rat chords up kybd ins w straddle (leave out middle note)
               - RH - rpt chords up kybd invs - 2/1 breakup (alternate bottom note with top 2)
               - RH - rat chords up kybd ins - 3/1 breakup  (alternate bottom note with top 3 for 4 note chords)
               - RH - make a run out of the chord

:20 (10 min) improv + independence
  :20 (10 min) improv over chords

:30 (10 min) ear training
  :30 (10 min) - learn and transcribe a song by ear

:40 (10 min) music theory
  :40 (10 min) - learn a song by sheet music

:50 (10 min) song practice
  :50 (10 min) - learn a song by heart
