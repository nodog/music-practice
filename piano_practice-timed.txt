timed piano practice - 7th draft
-----------------------------------
:00 (10 min) warm up + scale - stretch and breath before
  :00 ( 1 min) - weight lifting
  :01 ( 9 min) - DOY % 3
               = O: 2 octave scales
                 1: contradictory motion
                 2: hanon exercise DOM % 6

:10 (10 min) chord practice (all through inversions)
  :10 ( 6 min) DOY % 7 = O: full chords same inv
                         1: same direction arpeggio
                         2: run (no invs)
                         3: full chords diff inv
                         4: up/down arpeggio
                         5: full chords diff inv
                         6: reverse direction arpeggio
               DOM % 2  = starting direction O:right 1:left
               DOM % 10 = chord type = O:7th 1:triad 2:dom7th 3:aug 4:4th
                                       5:dim 6:6th   7:sus4   8:2nd 9:9th

  :16 ( 7 min) LH focus - chords for the day (song or blues)
               DOY % 16 =
                0 - LH/RH - sustain same triad chord
                1 - LH play root, then other triad notes alternating
                2 - LH Alberti bass - bottom, top, middle, top
                    (any triad inversion)
                3 - LH/RH - different triad inversions on each hand
                4 - LH swing bass
                    (pinky low 8vb root, followed by chord - any inversion)
                5 - LH arpeggios - root, 5th, 3rd-8va, 5th
                6 - LH/RH - rhythm across the notes of both hands
                7 - LH alternating bass
                    (swing bass, but alternate b/w root and 5th w/pinky)
                8 - LH 6th chord - break up with root, 5th, 6th, 5th
                9 - LH/RH - 7th chords both same
               10 - LH - boogie pattern - 1 8 3 4 4+ 5-8vb
               11 - LH - break up with root + 5th, m3rd, 3rd
               12 - LH/RH - 7th chords - LH 1-1 or 1-5, RH 3-5-7 or 3-7
               13 - LH 6th chord - break up with root, 6th, 5th, 6th
               14 - LH - classic boogie - 1 3 5 6 m7 6 5 3 1 - broken octaves
               15 - LH/RH - 9th chords - LH 1-1 or 1-5, RH 3-5-7-9 or 3-7-9

:23 ( 7 min) RH focus - improv + independence
  :23 ( 7 min) DOY % 9 =
                0 - RH - improv minor pentatonic
                1 - RH - rpt chord up the keyboard through inversions
                2 - RH - improv a melodic theme
                3 - RH - rpt chord up kybd invs w straddle
                         (leave out middle note)
                4 - RH - vocal melody
                5 - RH - rpt chord up kybd invs w 2/1 breakup
                         (alternate bottom note with top 2)
                6 - RH - improv mostly w/in the chord
                7 - RH - rpt chord up kybd invs w 3/1 breakup
                         (alternate bottom note with top 3 for 4 note chords)
                8 - RH - make a run out of the chord

:30 (10 min) ear training - learn and transcribe a song by ear

:40 (10 min) site reading

:50 (10 min) song practice - learn & perform a song by heart well
